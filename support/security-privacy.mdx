## Data we collect

Pierview collects and processes:

- **AI response data** — Outputs from AI models when we run your prompts. We extract brand mentions, sentiment, and citation URLs.
- **Account data** — Email, workspace info, and configuration (prompts, brands, scans).
- **Usage data** — How you use the product (e.g., scans run, reports generated) for product improvement.

We do **not** collect sensitive personal data from end users. We simulate queries and analyze public AI outputs.

## How we use it

- **Product delivery** — Run scans, generate reports, surface insights.
- **Improvement** — Improve accuracy, add models, refine metrics.
- **Support** — Respond to requests and troubleshoot issues.

## Security

- Data is transmitted over HTTPS.
- Access is authenticated and scoped to your workspace.
- We follow industry best practices for storage and access control. Details depend on your plan and data residency requirements.

## Privacy

- We do not sell your data.
- We do not use your prompt or scan data for training AI models.
- For full terms, see your agreement and privacy policy.
