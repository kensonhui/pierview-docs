## What is a pitch organization?

A **pitch organization** is a limited-access organization you can create to demo Pierview's AI visibility data to a prospective client. It works like a regular organization, with prompts, brand tracking, and visibility metrics, but it does not count toward your subscription seat.

Use pitch organizations when you want to:
- Show a prospect their current AI visibility before they sign up
- Run a comparison against their competitors as part of a sales pitch
- Validate Pierview's data with a client before onboarding them

---

## Creating a pitch organization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the organization switcher">
    Click your organization name in the top-left of the sidebar. Select **Add organization**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select 'Pitch'">
    Choose the **Pitch** type when prompted. You'll see your current pitch org usage (e.g., `1/3 used this month`).

    <Info>
      Pitch organizations are subject to a monthly limit based on your subscription plan. Once the limit is reached, you'll need to wait for the next calendar month or upgrade.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    Enter the prospective client's:
    - **Name**: e.g., the company name
    - **Domain**: e.g., `acmecorp.com` (used for citation and domain matching)
    - **Aliases** (optional), brand name variants to capture all mentions
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run a visibility scan">
    Switch into the pitch organization and run prompts as you would for any organization. Visibility, sentiment, and citation data will populate normally.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Pitch vs. standard organizations

| Feature | Pitch | Standard |
|---|---|---|
| Visibility, sentiment, citations | Yes | Yes |
| Counts toward billing | No | Yes |
| Monthly creation limit | Yes | No |
| Can be converted to standard | Yes | |

---

## Converting a pitch organization

When a prospective client decides to move forward, you can convert their pitch organization into a full organization. This preserves all existing data and configuration.

1. Open the pitch organization's settings (via the organization switcher → **Edit**)
2. Click **Convert to Full Organization**
3. Confirm, the organization will be added to your subscription and billed accordingly

<Warning>
  Converting a pitch organization is permanent. It will count toward your billing quantity and the `converted` status cannot be reverted.
</Warning>

---

## Limits and availability

- Monthly pitch org limits are set per workspace based on your subscription plan
- Limits reset at the start of each calendar month
- Creating pitch organizations requires workspace **Owner** or **Admin** role
- Pitch organizations require an active subscription to create
